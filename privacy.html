Last Updated: May 19, 2025

kiloBITz Solutions ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website kilobitz.com and submit a contact form.

Information We Collect

When you fill out the Contact Us form, we collect the following information:

  • Your name

Additionally, our form handling service (Basin) may capture:

We use the submitted information to:

We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes, and we do not use tracking cookies or behavioral analytics tools.

Data Security

Your data is transmitted securely via HTTPS and handled by trusted third-party services that follow industry best practices.

Your Rights (California Residents)
